DANCE TO YOUR OWN TUNE

MOVEMENT, MUSIC, AND MAGIC

Taught with a live pianist, these are lively but structured classes that develop person's physical skills and encourage creativity, expression and confidence.
Through participation in appropriate learning activities, children learn to:
  • to move with control, coordination and confidence
  • an awareness of their body and how it moves through space
  • a sensitivity to music, and
  • expression of emotions through movement.

JAZZ

All are taught by inspiring teachers who bring a wealth of professional performing and choreography experience to the studio. Offered at three levels, these classes develop jazz dance technique, including preparation for turning, leaps and isolation movements. Students will work to improve flexibility and strength and learn choreography in various styles including lyrical, commercial and musical theatre.

BOYS BALLET

These specialist non-syllabus classes focus on technique and choreography. Taught by a male LCDC teacher and accompanied by a live pianist, the classes develop invaluable skills including:

  • core strength
  • musicality, and
  • presentation skills.

STREET DANCE

In addition to the range of adult ballet classes offered at LCDC, there is a weekly street dance classes for 13 - 25 year olds. Broaden your dance experience with classes that energise and encourage self-expression, as well as developing musicality, flexibility and muscle strength. Experience classes that allow you to explore the total movement potential of the body in a fun and positive environment.
