About US

Lulworth Cove Drama Club are a local community group which is devloping a talent through different vocational courses for adult and children. This club is producing talented people of different ages. In this ages, there are also categories like 5-21 age counted as youth and above 21 age, they are adults. They are teaching different courses like acting, singing, dancing etc. and they will practise it on the school stage. Our school searching for talent student. Our school focus on talent students as well who have wise to perform activity. Our School is helping poor condition people also.
